CHARACTERISTICS

Mazda

The Mazda MX-5 Miata is a sporty roadster known for its agile handling and its 2.5-liter SkyActiv-G engine producing 186 HP. Additionally, it features a precise manual transmission and finely-tuned suspension to deliver an exciting driving experience.

Honda

The Honda Civic Si is a sporty compact sedan equipped with a 2.0-liter VTEC engine that generates 158 HP. This vehicle is known for its agility on the road and its well-designed interior with advanced technology, making it an ideal choice for enthusiastic drivers.

Subaru

The Subaru Outback is a crossover featuring symmetrical all-wheel drive and a 2.4-liter Boxer engine producing 260 HP. It stands out for its off-road capability, durability, and spacious interior. Ideal for outdoor adventures and challenging terrains.

Toyota

The Toyota Corolla Hybrid is a compact car with hybrid technology that combines a gasoline engine with an electric motor for a total power output of 208 HP. It offers excellent fuel efficiency and is known for its reliability and low maintenance cost.
